Ultrasonic Valve

An ultrasonic valve is a type of electronically controlled valve that uses ultrasonic sensors to detect fluid levels, flow, or presence, and then automatically opens or closes the valve based on that data. These valves are often used in smart water systems, irrigation, and automated liquid dispensing.

Thermal Enclosure

A thermal enclosure is a protective casing designed to maintain a controlled temperature around electronic components. It shields devices from extreme heat or cold, ensuring stable performance and preventing damage due to temperature fluctuations.

Gas Cooler

A gas cooler is a device used to reduce the temperature of gases by transferring heat to a cooling medium. It's essential in processes like emission monitoring and chemical plants where high gas temperatures can damage equipment.
